Hello World! I present to you, a 3D scene made by Zach Short entirely in Blender and rendered on a webpage using Three JS.

  • Mouse Scroll - Zoom in/out
  • Middle mouse button in & mouse move - smooth zoom
  • Left click & mouse move - rotate camera orbiting monkey
  • Right click & mouse move - shift camera position
  • This is a proof of concept, not an incredible product. The objects are not textured and lighting is just good enough to see the scene.
